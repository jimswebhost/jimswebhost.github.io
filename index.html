About Us


We are Leeds based Consultant ENT Surgeons, practicing at Spire Leeds Hospital, Nuffield Hospital Leeds and Leeds Teaching Hospitals NHS Trust. We offer a full range of adult and paediatric Otorhinolaryngology services to the good people of Leeds and further afield. Having undertaken higher surgical fellowships in Head & Neck Surgical Oncology and Thyroid surgery (JM) and Otology, Neurotology and Hearing Implants (TW) we provide subspecialist care in these areas. We undertake private healthcare for patients insured with all major private healthcare providers as well as self pay patients. Out NHS practice is delivered within Leeds Teaching Hospitals NHS Trust and via Choose and Book at Spire Leeds Hospital.

Conditions Treated

Both JM and TW provide general Ear, Nose and Throat services for both paediatric and adult patients including;

  • Tonsillitis, tonsillectomy and adenoidectomy
  • Hearing loss affecting children and adults
  • Glue ear and grommets
  • Tongue tie (frenulotomy)
  • Blocked noses and septoplasty
  • Rhinitis , sinusitis and nasal polyps
  • Hoarseness, throat symptoms and swallowing problems
  • Snoring
  • Blocked ears & ear wax
  • Bell’s plasy

New patient appointments are 30 minutes, follow up appointments are 15 minutes

Our specialisms

Sub-specialist conditions for Mr Moor include;

  • Thyroid and parathyroid disease
  • Neck lumps and bumps
  • Salivary gland disease
  • Head & Neck cancer
  • Problems with voice and singing.

For Mr Wilson, sub-specialist conditions include;

  • Chronic ear disease and mastoidectomy
  • Hearing disorders and hearing implants
  • Stapedectomy
  • Balance disorders
  • Septorhinoplasty
  • Tinnitus
